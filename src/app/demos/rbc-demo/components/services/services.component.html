Insight Edge

Benefits of Insight Edge for Small Business

Track shopping behaviour

Gain a deeper understanding of customer purchase trends to tailor inventory, promotions, and services based on actual spending patterns.

Reach your customers

Identify who your customers are, where they’re coming from, and how to better engage them through targeted marketing strategies.

Make informed real estate decisions

Use location-based insights to evaluate potential store locations, expansion opportunities, or site closures with greater confidence.

Optimize store hours and staffing

Align staffing and store hours with peak customer traffic to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance customer service.

Know your Business

Access easy-to-read dashboards that reveal business performance trends and help guide operational and strategic planning.

Compare your Locations

Benchmark store performance across multiple locations to uncover high-performers and areas needing improvement.
